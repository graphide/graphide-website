Recal Studios

Recalibrating the web.

Recal Studios is a small software development company, aiming to provide simple solutions to simple problems. Our products are made to be fun and easy to use, and have privacy at the core. We develop full-stack applications, with a primary focus on web apps.

We believe in openness, and this is reflected in our solutions. All of our software is open source, and have easy-to-read privacy policies. We only collect data that is critical to our software, and we never monitor or collect user behaviour. We store user data securely, and never sell it to third parties.
